# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, gender expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Showing empathy and patience toward new contributors.
- Giving constructive, specific feedback.
- Explaining domain concepts plainly when others ask.
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Insults or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Dismissing sincere beginner questions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, documentation, issues, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
